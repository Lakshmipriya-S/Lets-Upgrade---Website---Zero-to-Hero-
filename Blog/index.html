" ALL YOU GOT TO DO IS BALANCE YOUR PASSIONS, NOT YOUR TIME "

Work and life are INTEGRATED, the attitude is the key

Balance is not something you find, It is something you create. It's truly the perspective you carry on.

To create your own kind of a happy balance, keep moving on, and spend quality time on your choices.

Indeed we have got a lot of dreams to be achieved which may not be the same as our career goals. What do we do about it?

Change our goals and dreams? Make it just one?

NO, right? Even if we did, I'm sure, we won't end up being happy. After all, what is that we expect at the end of the day?, " Peace of Mind ".

From my experiences, " It's always about the quality we put into anything we do and never about the time we spent on it ".

If you are perfectly able to separate the time between your Work and Life, according to my opinion, "There's definitely something wrong about your work or your personal life".
